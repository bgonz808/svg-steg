⚠ Patched dependency. The renderer behind column A is oksvg (BSD-3), a project dependency used in the Go toolchain. Upstream oksvg has a stroke-width scaling bug: stroke width does not scale with the canvas, so strokes render too thick at low resolutions and too thin at high ones. svgsteg carries a small patch that fixes it, which is what makes the browser-vs-internal comparison meaningful.
